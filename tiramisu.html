Tiramisu

Images of the Finished Dish

Ingredients with Quantities

  • Ladyfingers: 24 pieces
  • Espresso coffee: 1 cup, cooled
  • Mascarpone cheese: 16 ounces
  • Granulated sugar: 1 cup

Cooking Instructions

  1. Dip ladyfingers in espresso and arrange a layer in a serving dish.
  2. In a bowl, mix mascarpone cheese and sugar until smooth.
  3. Spread a layer of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  4. Repeat layers and refrigerate until set.
