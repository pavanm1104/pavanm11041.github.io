Spaghetti

Images of the Finished Dish

Ingredients with Quantities

  • Spaghetti noodles: 400g
  • Tomato sauce: 2 cups
  • Meatballs: 1 cup
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook spaghetti noodles until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil and cook meatballs until browned.
  3. Combine cooked spaghetti, tomato sauce, and meatballs.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious spaghetti!
