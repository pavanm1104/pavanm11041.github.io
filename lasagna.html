Lasagna

Images of the Finished Dish

Ingredients with Quantities

  • Lasagna noodles: 9 sheets
  • Ground beef: 1 pound
  • Ricotta cheese: 2 cups
  • Mozzarella cheese: 2 cups, shredded

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown ground beef in a pan and season with your favorite spices.
  3. In a baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, ground beef, and mozzarella cheese.
  4. Repeat the layers and bake until cheese is melted and bubbly.
