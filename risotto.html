Risotto

Images of the Finished Dish

Ingredients with Quantities

  • Arborio rice: 1 cup
  • Chicken or vegetable broth: 4 cups
  • White wine: 1/2 cup
  • Onion: 1, finely chopped

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a pan, sauté chopped onions in olive oil until translucent.
  2. Add Arborio rice and cook until lightly toasted.
  3. Pour in white wine and stir until absorbed.
  4. Gradually add chicken or vegetable broth, stirring continuously until rice is cooked.
